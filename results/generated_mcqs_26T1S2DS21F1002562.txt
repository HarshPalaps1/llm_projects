## MCQ 1
Question: What is the duration of the IIT Madras BS in Data Science and Applications exam?
A) 1 hour
B) 2 hours
C) 3 hours
D) 4 hours
Correct Answer: B) 2 hours

## MCQ 2
Question: What is the reporting time for the exam at the test centre?
A) 12:00 P.M. IST
B) 01:00 P.M. IST
C) 02:00 P.M. IST
D) 03:00 P.M. IST
Correct Answer: B) 01:00 P.M. IST

## MCQ 3
Question: What type of attire are candidates expected to wear to the exam?
A) Casual wear
B) Professional attire
C) Sports wear
D) Traditional wear

## MCQ 4
Question: What is the consequence of answering subjects not eligible for the candidate?
A) The answers will be considered for evaluation
B) The answers will be counted towards the final score
C) The answers will not be considered or counted
D) The candidate will be disqualified
Correct Answer: C) The answers will not be considered or counted
